# awesome_chocolates
## Objective:

Developed a dynamic finance KPI dashboard for Sunbank to track monthly and year-to-date sales performance against targets using Excel and Power BI. The dashboard visualized trends, individual sales performance, team comparisons, and provided actionable metrics to support strategic decision-making.

## Skills Utilized:

- Data analysis and visualization in Excel.
- Power BI dashboard creation and DAX formula design.

## Approach:

- Consolidated YTD and monthly sales data for multiple salespersons and teams in Excel.
- Created interactive visualizations in Power BI, including bar charts, variance indicators, sparklines, and team/individual performance matrices.
- Integrated dynamic filters and automated variance calculations to highlight outliers and trends.
- Designed commentary panels to summarize insights and trends for broader context.

## Key Insights:

- Seven out of fourteen monthly targets were reached, with total actual sales ($5M) nearly matching annual targets ($5M).
- Sales variance was minor overall (+$6K), but significant positive and negative variances exist among individual performers.
- Sales actual saw its steepest increase early in the year and showed downward trends toward year-end.
- Target achievement was uneven, with positive outliers (Gigi Bohling, Barr Faughny) and areas needing attention (Wilone O’Kielt, Gunar Cockshoot)

## Recommendations

- Address negative variance outliers with targeted coaching for underperforming salespersons.
- Analyze November-January sales declines further and establish corrective action plans.
- Refine KPI targets to align monthly goals with prior performance trends and market shifts.
- Expand dashboard to accommodate real-time analytics and predictive modeling for future planning.

## Impact:

- Enhanced visibility into sales target achievement and team/individual performance for management.
- Enabled strategic interventions through clear, actionable insights on sales trends and variances.
- Fostered data-driven culture and improved accountability across teams with transparent reporting.
- Accelerated decision-making with automated, interactive financial KPI dashboards.
